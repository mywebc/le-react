### Rate评分
<p>评分组件。</p>

## 基本使用
<p>最简单的用法。</p>

<Playground>
    <div className="basis">
       <Rate />
    </div>
</Playground>


## 半星
<p>支持选中半星。</p>

<Playground>
    <div className="basis">
       <Rate allowHalf/>
    </div>
</Playground>

## 默认值
<p>可以设置默认值。</p>

<Playground>
    <div className="basis">
       <Rate defaultValue={2}/>
       <Rate defaultValue={2.5} allowHalf/>
    </div>
</Playground>

## 只读
<p>只读，无法进行鼠标交互。</p>

<Playground>
    <div className="basis">
       <Rate defaultValue={4} disabled/>
       <Rate defaultValue={1.5} allowHalf disabled/>
    </div>
</Playground>

## 清除
<p>支持允许或者禁用清除。</p>

<Playground>
    <div className="basis">
       <Rate defaultValue={4} allowClear/>
       <Rate defaultValue={1.5} allowHalf allowClear/>
    </div>
</Playground>

## 自定义字符
<p>可以将星星替换为其他字符。</p>

<Playground>
    <div className="basis">
       <Rate defaultValue={4} character={[
          <Icon name="smile-fill" />,
          <Icon name="smile-fill" />,
          <Icon name="smile-fill" />,
       ]}/>
       <Rate defaultValue={1.5} allowHalf allowClear character={[
          <Icon name="vip-fill" />,
          <Icon name="vip-fill" />,
          <Icon name="vip-fill" />,
          <Icon name="vip-fill" />,
          <Icon name="vip-fill" />,
       ]}/>
    </div>
</Playground>
